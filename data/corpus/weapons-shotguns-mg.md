# Shotguns and machine guns

## Shotguns
Shotguns are devastating at close range and useless past it. They pay a large
$900 reward per kill, so a lucky eco shotgun round can swing a team's economy.

- **Nova ($1050)** — pump-action, reliable one-to-two-shot kills up close, the
  most common eco shotgun.
- **XM1014 ($2000)** — fully automatic, high fire rate for clearing tight
  spaces, but expensive.
- **MAG-7 ($1300, CT)** — short-range powerhouse, ideal for CTs holding a close
  angle on an anti-eco round.
- **Sawed-Off ($1100, T)** — wide spread and brutal point-blank damage, the T
  counterpart for ambushing at a choke.

Shotguns are a classic way to hold a tight site corner against a forced push:
the attacker has to round the corner into a one-shot weapon.

## Machine guns
- **M249 ($5200)** — a 100-round belt with heavy recoil; rarely bought because
  the price could fund a rifle plus utility instead.
- **Negev ($1700)** — extremely inaccurate at first, but the spray tightens as
  you keep firing, so it can wall off a choke or smoke with a wall of bullets.
  It is mostly a fun or meme anti-eco pick rather than a serious buy.
