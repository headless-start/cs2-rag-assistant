# Mirage callouts and overview

Mirage is the most-played competitive map and the one most players learn first.
It is built around strong mid control: the team that owns mid can pressure both
bomb sites.

## Mid
Key spots are **window** (the elevated CT room overlooking mid), **top mid**
near the T side, **catwalk** leading toward A, **connector** linking mid into A,
and **jungle** just outside A site. CTs usually smoke window or top mid to deny
the AWP duel; Ts smoke window to take catwalk and threaten A from connector.

## A site
The bombsite is approached from **palace** (also called ramp/balcony on the T
right) and from **ramp** plus **tetris/stairs** on the left after catwalk. On
the site itself are **ticket booth**, **firebox**, **default plant**, **sandwich
/ CT spawn** behind, and **triple** boxes. A standard A execute smokes CT and
jungle, flashes over, and trades into the site.

## B site
B is reached through **apartments** (apps), entered from the T side and dropping
into the site, and from **B short / catwalk** via mid. Inside are **market**
(the building overlooking the site), **bench**, **van**, and **kitchen**. CTs
hold from market and short; a B execute smokes market and short and floods out
of apartments.

## How rounds tend to play
Mid control dictates everything: take mid and A and B both open up via connector
and short. T sides often fake one site with utility and swing to the other,
while CTs rotate through mid and the connector behind the sites.
