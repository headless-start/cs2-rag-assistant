# Inferno callouts and overview

Inferno is a utility-heavy map of tight chokes and close angles, which makes
grenades and coordinated executes more important than raw aim. CTs can hold with
very few players if their utility is good, so Ts must spend smokes and molotovs
to take ground.

## Banana (the B approach)
**Banana** is the long curved corridor from T spawn to B. **Car** sits near the
top, with **sandbags / logs** and **CT** beyond it. Control of banana is the
single most fought-over space on the map; CTs molotov and HE it to deny early
pushes, Ts smoke the top and molotov car to clear it.

## B site
Entered from the top of banana. Inside are **first oranges** and **second
oranges** (the crates), **fountain**, **dark**, **new box**, **coffin**, and
**sandbags**. CTs hold from coffin, fountain, and near new box; a B execute
smokes CT and dark and floods from banana.

## A site
Reached through **apartments** (apps) and through **mid** to **short**. On and
around the site are **pit**, **graveyard**, **arch**, **balcony**, **library**,
and **boiler**. The A push usually needs smokes on CT, arch, and library plus a
molotov to clear pit, because the defender angles are numerous and close.

## How rounds tend to play
T sides commit utility to win either banana or apartments, then trade into the
site. CTs play for early picks with molotovs and retake with saved utility.
Mid links the two halves of the map and lets CTs rotate or Ts fake.
