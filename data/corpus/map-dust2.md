# Dust II callouts and overview

Dust II is the most iconic map in Counter-Strike and the most aim-driven of the
active pool, with long sightlines that reward rifles and the AWP. Its layout is
simple to read, which is why it is a favorite for newer players.

## A side
**Long A** (also long doors / blue) is the wide stretch from T toward A site,
passing **pit** and **the corner** and opening onto the site near **goose** and
**default plant**. **Catwalk** (cat / short A) drops from near mid onto the
site over **A short**. CTs hold from **A site**, **goose**, and **CT spawn**;
the AWP duel down long is one of the defining fights on the map.

## Mid
**Mid doors** split the map; **lower tunnels** and **upper tunnels** run from T
toward B, and **catwalk** branches to A. **Mid to B** through the doors and
**xbox** in the middle are key. Controlling mid lets Ts fake or split and lets
CTs push for picks.

## B side
The **tunnels** (upper and lower) feed Ts into **B site**. On site are **B
platform**, **window** (overlooking from CT), **back plat**, and **doors**. CTs
hold from window, doors, and back site; a B execute floods out of tunnels behind
smokes and flashes.

## How rounds tend to play
Because sightlines are long and open, Dust II rewards crisp aim and AWP control
more than heavy utility. Ts pick a side to commit numbers, use smokes on long
corners or CT, and trade through the choke.
