# Armor, defuse kit, and Zeus

## Kevlar and helmet
Armor reduces the damage you take and removes the flinch that throws off your
aim when you are shot, so it is one of the highest-value purchases in the game.

- **Kevlar vest ($650)** — protects the torso and legs.
- **Kevlar + helmet ($1000)** — adds head protection, which is what stops a
  pistol or SMG headshot from being an instant kill. Rifles like the AK still
  one-shot a helmeted head, but against pistols the helmet is decisive on the
  pistol round.

Buying armor is usually prioritized over a third grenade, because surviving the
first contact is worth more than extra utility.

## Defuse kit ($400, CT only)
A defuse kit cuts bomb defuse time from 10 seconds to 5 seconds. That five
seconds is often the difference between a successful retake and losing the
round, so CTs with spare money should always carry a kit. Picking up a kit
dropped by a dead teammate works too.

## Zeus x27 ($200)
The Zeus is a single-use taser with very short range that kills instantly on a
direct hit and refunds nothing on a miss. It is bought on ecos to guarantee a
weapon steal, to break an aggressive push at a choke, or as a cheeky close-range
finisher. After firing it must recharge before it can be used again.
