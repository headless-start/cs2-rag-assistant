# Submachine guns

SMGs are cheap, accurate while moving, and pay a larger kill reward ($600 each,
except the P90 at $300), which makes them the standard half-buy and anti-eco
weapon. They shred unarmored opponents up close but lose to rifles at range.

## MAC-10 ($1050, T) and MP9 ($1250, CT)
The cheapest run-and-gun SMGs, with very high fire rates and excellent mobility.
They are the go-to weapons for the second round of a half after winning the
pistol, and for aggressive eco pushes.

## MP7 ($1500) and MP5-SD ($1500)
Mid-priced all-rounders with good control and damage. The MP5-SD is silenced,
hiding tracers and muffling the shot, and many players prefer it for holding
angles. The two are functionally interchangeable; the choice is preference.

## UMP-45 ($1200)
Hits hard per shot with strong armor penetration and good range for an SMG,
making it a reliable anti-eco pick that can punch above its price.

## P90 ($2350)
A 50-round magazine, high fire rate, and easy spray make the P90 forgiving but
expensive for an SMG, and it pays only $300 per kill. It is a comfort pick for
close, chaotic sites.

## PP-Bizon ($1400)
A 64-round magazine with low damage; a niche weapon for spraying through smokes
or holding a choke where volume of fire matters more than precision.
