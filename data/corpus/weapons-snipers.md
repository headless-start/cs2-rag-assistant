# Sniper rifles

## AWP ($4750)
The AWP is a one-shot kill to the chest, stomach, or head at any range, which
makes it the single most impactful weapon in CS2. It is slow to fire, slow to
reload, and slows the player heavily while scoped, so positioning and timing
matter more than reflexes. A team usually runs one dedicated AWPer because the
price eats most of a round's budget. Quick-scoping and no-scoping work up close,
but the weapon is built for holding long angles and gathering early picks.

## SSG 08 / Scout ($1700)
The SSG 08, known as the Scout, is the budget sniper. It only one-shots to the
head, but it is cheap, light, and lets the player move quickly while scoped,
making it strong on eco and force rounds and for aggressive jump-shots. It is a
common way to keep AWP-style picks alive when the economy is thin.

## Auto-snipers: SCAR-20 and G3SG1
The SCAR-20 (CT) and G3SG1 (T) are fully automatic scoped rifles that do heavy
damage per shot. They are very expensive and slow the player down, so they are
a situational anti-eco or stalemate pick rather than a default buy, and the
community generally considers them clumsy compared with the AWP.
