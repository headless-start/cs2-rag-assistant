# The bomb and the objective

## The C4
One terrorist starts each round carrying the C4 bomb. It can be planted only on
a designated bombsite (A or B). If the carrier dies, the bomb drops and any
terrorist can pick it up and carry it; planting it remains the surest path to
the round win and to the T economy.

## Planting
Planting takes about 3.2 seconds of holding still on the site, during which the
player is vulnerable, so teams clear and screen the plant spot with utility
first. A good plant position is one the team can defend in the post-plant — for
example, planting where teammates' crossfires cover the bomb.

## After the plant
Once planted, the 40-second bomb timer is the only clock that matters; the round
no longer ends when the round timer expires. The Ts win if it detonates, so
their job becomes denying the defuse for 40 seconds. Planting also locks in the
$800 team plant bonus even if the round is later lost.

## Defusing
CTs defuse by holding still next to the bomb for 10 seconds, or 5 seconds with a
defuse kit. The defuse can be interrupted by taking the CT off the bomb, and
must then be restarted. Because of this, CTs usually clear the site or pin the
Ts down before committing to the defuse, and a "fake defuse" (starting and
stopping) can bait a hidden T into revealing themselves.

## Why the objective drives the game
Every economy and positioning decision ultimately serves the bomb: the Ts'
whole game plan is getting it down and defending it, and the CTs' is denying the
plant or surviving long enough to defuse.
