# Ancient callouts and overview

Ancient is a Mesoamerican-themed map added in the CS2 era pool. It is fairly
compact with a strong, contestable mid, and it rewards utility-based executes
onto sites with limited entrances.

## Mid
Mid is the connective tissue of the map, linking T spawn toward both sites via
**mid**, **cave**, and the **donut** room. Winning mid lets Ts split A or feed
into B, and lets CTs hold aggressively or rotate quickly.

## A site
Approached through **main** and from mid via **donut**. On site are **temple**,
**CT**, and the default plant near the central pillar. CTs hold from CT and
temple; an A execute smokes CT and clears the close angles with molotov and
flashes.

## B site
Reached through **ramp** and the connecting corridor from mid. The site is
tighter, with **CT** and the back of the site as the holding spots, and a single
main choke that defenders can molotov and smoke to stall a push.

## How rounds tend to play
With limited entrances per site, Ancient is an execute map: T sides save utility
to commit fully to one site with smokes and flashes, while CTs lean on molotovs
and crossfires to delay and on saved utility to retake.
